
## **Assignment 1: Reflection Essay (Due end of Week 1)**

**Instructions:**

Write a 1-2 page reflection answering:

1. **In your own words, what is data engineering?** (No copying from lessons!)
2. **Pick one company/app you use daily.** Describe how you think data engineering works behind the scenes. Consider:
   - What data is collected?
   - Where might it be stored?
   - How is it processed?
   - Who uses the results?
3. **What excites you about data engineering?** What concerns you?
